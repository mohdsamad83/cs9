VINS Overview

The Vicharanashala internship is a rigorous research-oriented program at IIT Ropar designed to involve students in high-impact projects spanning technology and social innovation. It provides hands-on experience in building scalable solutions while being mentored by leading academic experts.
VINS stands for Vicharanashala Internship Network System. it is our proprietary ecosystem that manages candidate selection, project allocation, and the collaborative lifecycle of the internship program.
VINS is divided into Selection, Onboarding, Execution, and Certification phases. Badges are awarded based on milestone achievements, technical proficiency demonstrated, and contribution levels within your respective team.
The internship is primarily for current undergraduate and postgraduate students. However, alumni within one year of graduation may apply for specific technical roles depending on current project requirements.
While conducted under the laboratory's umbrella at IIT Ropar, this program has distinct selection criteria and operational flows tailored for our specific research labs. It is complementary but separate from the institute-wide general summer internships.
Leave requests are handled through the ViBe platform. Academic commitments are always prioritized, provided you inform your project lead at least 24 hours in advance and ensure your critical deliverables are transitioned to team members.

Need direct assistance?

Our support team is available during lab hours to help with specific onboarding or platform issues.
